Molding and Casting

I forgot to take a picture of this project. But I was able to get experience with parafen wax and plaster molding.

For my plater mold I tried to make a simple bunny face design out of plaster.

I think it would have worked out better if I had 3D printed an object to make an indentation into the sand, instead of trying to pack the sand down by hand.

I also messed around with trying to get food coloring designs in a wax mold. It was tricky though because the dye would cool the wax down and harden it.
